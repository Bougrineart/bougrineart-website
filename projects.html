Projects

Here, you’ll find an overview of my most significant projects from recent times. This collection is a blend of commissioned works and personal pieces, which were created by my creative exploration. Each piece reflects a unique journey and a different aspect of my artistic expression.

"Wet Green"

"Wet Green" marked the moment I discovered my artistic style. The rich details of nature and bold colors reflect my philosophy of life — vibrant, expressive, and full of energy.

"Rainy Drive"

"Rainy Drive" was created during a rainy German winter when my mood mirrored the weather. I experimented with cold tones, special lighting effects, and the illusion of real raindrops to explore the possibilities of capturing this atmosphere.

"Waldsee"

"Waldsee" (which means Forest Lake in German) is one of my favorite paintings because of its intricate green details and the natural warm colors, which remind me of the warm European summer days from my childhood.

"Night Shift"

"Night Shift" is a project planned for a local burger restaurant, "Patties and Berries," by a friend, marking the first time I introduced my art to my community. This painting also contains many hidden details that I personally identify with.

Future Projects
